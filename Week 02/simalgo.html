simple step-by-step algorithm or pseudocode for making a cup of coffee 

1. Boil water
   a. Fill kettle with water
   b. Place kettle on stove
   c. Turn on stove
2. Prepare coffee
   a. Grind coffee beans (if whole)
   b. Add coffee grounds to filter
3. Pour water
   a. Wait for water to boil
   b. Carefully pour hot water over grounds
4. Serve
   a. Remove filter
   b. Pour coffee into mug
   c. Add milk/sugar (optional)
