Peak Majesty Hiking in the Swiss Alps

This premium hiking package is designed for those who want to experience the raw grandeur of the Bernese Oberland and Valais regions without sacrificing comfort. From the thundering waterfalls of Lauterbrunnen to the iconic silhouette of the Matterhorn, this itinerary focuses on high-altitude "balcony trails" that offer maximum views with strategically planned ascents.

Package Details

Duration: 9 Days / 8 Nights

Activity Level: Moderate to Challenging (Daily hikes of 5–8 miles with elevation gains up to 3,000 ft)

Group Size: Maximum 12 travelers

Accommodations: Hand-picked 4-star "Alps-chic" hotels and one night in a luxury mountain lodge.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Zurich & Transfer to Grindelwald

Arrive at Zurich Airport (ZRH). Meet your guide and enjoy a scenic 2.5-hour transfer to the charming village of Grindelwald, nestled at the foot of the Eiger North Face.

Check into your hotel and enjoy a welcome dinner featuring traditional Swiss cuisine.

Day 2: The Eiger Trail & Panoramic Views

Take the Eiger Express gondola to the Eigergletscher station.

Hike the famous Eiger Trail, walking directly beneath the imposing North Face of the Eiger.

Descend to Alpiglen, enjoying breathtaking views of the Grindelwald valley.

Day 3: Mürren & the Schilthorn

Travel to Lauterbrunnen, the valley of 72 waterfalls.

Ascend to the car-free village of Mürren via cable car.

Take the revolving Piz Gloria cable car to the summit of the Schilthorn, famous for its 360-degree views and James Bond connection.

Day 4: Bachalpsee Lake & First Cliff Walk

Ride the gondola to Grindelwald-First.

Hike to the stunning Bachalpsee lake, known for its crystal-clear waters and reflections of the surrounding peaks.

Experience the thrilling First Cliff Walk by Tissot, a metal walkway clinging to the cliff face.

Day 5: Transfer to Zermatt & Matterhorn Views

Enjoy a scenic train journey from Grindelwald to Zermatt, the village at the foot of the Matterhorn.

Check into your hotel and take a leisurely stroll through the charming streets of Zermatt.

Day 6: Gornergrat Railway & Riffelsee Lake

Ride the historic Gornergrat cogwheel railway to the summit, offering unparalleled views of the Matterhorn and surrounding glaciers.

Hike from Rotenboden to Riffelsee lake, famous for its perfect reflection of the Matterhorn.

Day 7: 5-Seenweg (Five Lakes Trail)

Take the Sunnegga funicular and gondola to Blauherd.

Hike the 5-Seenweg, a spectacular trail passing five alpine lakes, each offering unique perspectives of the Matterhorn.

Day 8: Relaxation or Optional Hike

Enjoy a free day to relax in Zermatt or choose an optional hike, such as the hike to the Hörnlihütte (base camp for Matterhorn climbers).

Farewell dinner celebrating your Swiss Alps adventure.

Day 9: Departure

Transfer from Zermatt to Zurich Airport (ZRH) for your departure.
